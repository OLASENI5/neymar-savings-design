How the money works
31
A 31-day contribution cycle
Each customer saves across a cycle of 31 days, matching the daily contribution card in use today.
1
The first day is the platform slot
The first saving of each cycle goes to Neymar Savings as commission. It is shown clearly so every customer sees it.
2
Two contributions always stay
A customer can withdraw at any time but must leave 2 daily contributions. On NGN 1,000 daily, that is NGN 2,000 kept in the account.
₦
Digital payments settle to your account
Customers can also pay by card or transfer. All money settles directly into the company bank account.
%
Everything is transparent
Every deposit, withdrawal and platform slot is recorded. Customers and staff can see the full history at any time.
