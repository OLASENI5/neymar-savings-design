Change 3
Self sign-up with approval
A customer can register themselves. They enter their details and location, set a 4-digit PIN, and land in a pending state. They cannot save or withdraw until an admin approves. At approval the system suggests agents at the customer's location and the admin confirms one, so every self-registered customer is verified and bound to the right agent. Agents can still onboard customers directly in the field, bound immediately.

Change 4 . New business rule
End-of-cycle account closure
At the end of a 31-day cycle, a customer may close the account and withdraw everything. On closure the two-contribution minimum is waived. The day-1 platform commission stays with Neymar Savings and is not refundable, as it was collected at the start of the cycle.

Summary of v3 changes

  • Customer notifications: in-app bell and notifications screen, delivered by push. Reminders for due, missed and non-payment, plus account notices. SMS remains outside Version 1.
  • Transaction PIN reduced to 4 digits, protected by server-side lockout after repeated wrong attempts.
  • Self sign-up added, always landing in a pending state. At approval the system suggests agents at the customer's location and the admin confirms one, binding the customer. Agents can also onboard customers directly in the field, bound immediately.
  • End-of-cycle closure: withdraw all savings with the floor waived, day-1 commission retained and non-refundable.
